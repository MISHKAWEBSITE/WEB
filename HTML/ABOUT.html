NDA!

We don’t store client data. Ever. We don’t need your name, phone number, ID, or accounts. Our systems are built in a way that makes data leaks technically impossible.
There’s simply nothing to steal.

Anonymity isn’t a side effect — it’s a core value. We don’t track user activity, don’t link sessions, don’t set cookies, and don’t run analytics scripts.
Even our internal systems are designed to leave no trace.

Security, for us, isn’t just encryption (though of course, that’s covered).
It’s about trust by design: no unnecessary data, decentralized logic, and control that stays in your hands.
